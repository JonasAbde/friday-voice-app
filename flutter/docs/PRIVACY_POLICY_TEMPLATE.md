# Privacy Policy for Friday Voice Assistant

**Last Updated:** February 6, 2026

## Introduction

Friday Voice Assistant ("Friday", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**Please read this privacy policy carefully.** If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using Friday:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number
- **Business Information:** Company name, business type, tax/VAT number
- **Customer Data:** Names, contact information, appointment details (when you add them)
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through third-party providers (we do not store credit card numbers)

### Voice Data

- **Voice Recordings:** When you use voice commands, your voice is temporarily recorded and transmitted to our AI processing service (OpenAI) for transcription and command processing
- **Transcriptions:** Text versions of your voice commands are stored to improve accuracy and provide service history
- **Retention:** Voice recordings are deleted within 30 days unless you choose to save them for review

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, commands executed, app performance metrics
- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, app crashes, error reports

### Calendar and Contact Data

- **Calendar Access:** Read and write access to calendars you grant permission to (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar)
- **Contact Information:** Names and contact details you add manually or import

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services:**
   - Process voice commands
   - Schedule appointments
   - Create invoices
   - Manage customer relationships

2. **Improve Friday:**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Train AI models (with anonymized data only)
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication:**
   - Send service updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Notify about important changes

4. **Legal Compliance:**
   - Comply with laws and regulations
   - Enforce our terms of service
   - Protect rights and safety

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted third parties only as necessary:

- **OpenAI:** Voice transcription and AI processing (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Google:** Calendar synchronization (if you connect Google Calendar)
- **Cloud Hosting:** Secure data storage (AWS/Google Cloud)
- **Analytics:** App performance monitoring (anonymized data)

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to advertisers
- Share your voice recordings with third parties (except OpenAI for processing)
- Use your data for marketing without consent

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes
- Requests from government authorities
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Prevention of fraud or security threats

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Storage:** Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
- **Access Control:** Strict internal access policies
- **Regular Audits:** Security reviews and penetration testing

**However, no system is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your data
- Export your data in machine-readable format

### Correction
- Update or correct your personal information
- Modify incorrect data in the app

### Deletion
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- Delete specific voice recordings or transcriptions

### Opt-Out
- Disable voice command features (use text input instead)
- Revoke calendar and contact permissions
- Unsubscribe from promotional emails

**To exercise these rights:**
- Email: support@rendetalje.dk
- In-app: Settings → Privacy → Data Management

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Voice Recordings:** Deleted after 30 days (or immediately upon request)
- **Transcriptions:** Retained for service history (deletable on request)
- **Invoices/Appointments:** Retained for tax/legal purposes (minimum 5 years in Denmark)
- **Deleted Account:** All data permanently deleted within 90 days

## Children's Privacy
